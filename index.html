Van Allen Radiation Belt Energy Harvester

A 25,700-net orbital constellation harvesting energetic particles from Earth's Van Allen belt through four mechanisms — including a living radiotrophic melanized fungal layer that converts ionizing radiation to electricity via a melanin semiconductor electron-sorting mechanism. Self-repairing. Self-replicating. Radiation-enhanced rather than radiation-degraded. Spider drones with gecko-pad feet service the nets permanently, drawing power from the nets they maintain. 2.33 GW continuous clean power to ground via 1,064 nm laser downlink.
